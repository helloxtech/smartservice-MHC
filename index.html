About the Solution

Many Manufactured Home Communities face complex regulatory, operational, and tenant engagement challenges. SmartService offers a tailored, low-code based property management system that integrates seamlessly with Microsoft Power Platform and Dataverse.

Our system is designed to improve intake workflows, compliance monitoring, service requests, and owner transparency through a secure and auditable platform architecture.

Features

  • Tenant and Potential Tenant Portal: Application intake, rent payments, maintenance requests, and communications.
  • Investor (Co-owner) Dashboard: Transparent operational and financial reporting tailored for community stakeholders.
  • Property Manager Tools: Model-driven app functionality on Dataverse for efficient operations and compliance tracking.
  • Secure, Privacy-Aware Workflow Automation and Audit-Ready Data Governance.
  • Scalable and adaptable to multiple communities with reusable governance frameworks.
